13 reasons



High school student Clay Jensen receives a mysterious package in the mail with seven cassette tapes recorded by Hannah Baker, a classmate who recently died by suicide.
As he listens to the tapes, he learns that there is a page for each person to whom Hannah attributes her reasons for killing herself.

  1. "You're not going, neither now nor alive. Why didn't you tell me I was alive?"
  2. "If you listen to this tape now, you are one of the reasons I am no longer alive"
